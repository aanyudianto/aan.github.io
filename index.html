About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Manchester working on data-driven shape optimization and hybrid RANS-LES turbulence modeling. My research focuses on improving turbulence onset prediction in separated flows using advanced CFD and machine learning methods.

Research Interests

  • Flow separation and turbulence onset
  • Bayesian optimization for shape design
  • OpenFOAM & adjoint-based optimization
